Kexin (Kathy) is a third-year PhD student in the Department of Psychology. She is broadly interested in the interaction between language and cognition, with a particular focus on number language and numerical cognition. She works with Jiayi and Dedre Gentner on projects investigating how children learn the meanings of the first few number words (e.g., two, three, four).
