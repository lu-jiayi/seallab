Jiayi, Anzi, and Shirley attended and presented at the 39th Human Sentence Processing (HSP) conference, which took place at MIT from 3/26 to 28, 2026. 

Shirley presented her poster "Island Satiates without Change in Backgroundedness". Anzi presented "Could 40% mean 10%? Adding an additional layer of uncertainty to the RSA framework", work done in collaboration with Carolyn Anderson and Grusha Prasad.
