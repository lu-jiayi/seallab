Jiayi, Anzi, and Kexin (along with Eszter and Jiaming from the [ExMean Group](https://sites.northwestern.edu/xmean/)) attended the 4th Experiments in Linguistic Meaning (ELM) conference, which took place at the University of Pennsylvania from 6/10 to 12, 2026. 

Eszter presented a talk (co-authored with Jiayi, Anzi, and Jiaming), titled "Evidence for Adaptation in Scalar Implicature Calculation". 

Kexin presented a talk (co-authored with Lance Rips), titled "Why isn’t 'twenty-eight' 'ten ten eight'?"
